Rabat

In Rabat, students can study at Qalam wa Lawh Centre for Arabic Studies

There is only the option to study for a semester here, but both semester one and two are available

Students will only study Arabic during their stay here, and will focus on their other subject(s) in their other semester
Students can choose to stay in dorms, with a guest family, or to find their own private accommodation

There are 4 hours of Fusha class per day, with an additional 2 hours of class available to those who wish to also study Moroccan dialect

The school offers regular trips to Fes, Casablanca, Chefchouan, Marrakesh and the Sahara
