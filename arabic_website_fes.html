Fes


In Fes, students can study at the Arabic Language Institue Fes (ALIF)

There is only the option to study for a semester here, but both semester one and two are available

Students will only study Arabic during their stay here, and will focus on their other subject(s) in their other semester
Students can choose to stay in the ALIF residence, with a guest family, in a hotel, or to find their own private accommodation

There are 4 hours of Fusha class per day, though further dialect class is available

The school offers regular field trips to different parts of Fes and the nearby cities of Meknes and Rabat
